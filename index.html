SubVivah – Children’s Privacy & Protection Policy

1. Age Restriction

SubVivah is strictly intended for adults only. Individuals under 18 years of age are not permitted to create an account or use our services. This restriction applies equally to all genders.

2. No Collection of Children’s Data

We do not knowingly collect, store, or process personal information of individuals under 18. If a minor attempts to register, the account will be removed and all associated data permanently deleted.

3. Age Verification Measures

  • Mandatory date of birth declaration
  • Face verification during registration
  • Manual review in suspicious cases
  • Permanent suspension for false age claims

4. Reporting Underage Users

If you believe someone under 18 is using SubVivah, please report immediately:

Email: info@robustrix.com

5. Legal Compliance

SubVivah complies with Google Play policies and applicable child protection laws. Our platform is strictly categorized as Adults Only (18+).
